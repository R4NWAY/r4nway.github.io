“Premium non nocere”


I strive to operate under the guiding principle “first, do no harm” and I seek to safeguard the welfare and rights of those with whom I interact.
It is at utmost importance to me to nurture this trust and respect and protect the privacy of people, who trusted me and opened up about their lives,
their pains, often their traumas. I want to ensure that you are absolutely comfortable and confident that you are not, cannot, and will not be identified and that you enjoy a better, healthier, and happier life undisturbed, now and later on.

Lucyna Milanowska-Sigrist
