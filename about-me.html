My mission

Uphold trust in human greatness.

I strive to help people rediscover firm ground and re-direct their long-suppressed potential towards a life they longed for and meant to live. With clarity and confidence in their choices, they inspire others to choose life with meaning and purpose.

In this way, we collectively outreach and outpace negativity, fear, and a sense of powerlessness that drains and weakens us. So that we, our children, and succeeding generations stop holding the breath but stand up straight calmly and confidently, look straight in the eye of the ones we deeply care about, and ones who wish to do harm, trusting ourselves, trusting our instincts, and knowing we are doing the right thing.
